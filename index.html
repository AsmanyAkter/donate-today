Our Donate Plan

We are trying to fill up the basic needs of flood-affected people. For this reason, we will provide food, shelter, and education.

Food for a family

Flood-affected areas people struggled to access drinking water and food. So we plan to feed 5,000 families in the Sunamganj district.

Shelter for a family

Many people lost their houses because of the massive floods, and some of them are really poor. We are planning to help around 800 families.

Education for a child

We are planning to reconstruct all the educational institutes so that students can go back to their classes without any delay. We need financial help to do that.
